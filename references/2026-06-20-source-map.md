## Query patterns that worked well

- Use exact-phrase searches around official announcement titles when broad searches are noisy.
- For social/X/Reddit, search the canonical item/post URL first, then cite the post URL and label the signal as thin if direct extraction is partial.
- When June 19-20 results are sparse, widen to 24-72h and say so explicitly instead of pretending the window was denser than it was.
- Prefer primary sources and item pages; avoid turning search snippets into settled claims.
